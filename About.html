BEN ACADEMY OF DANCE

The goal of Ben Dance Academy is to provide a range of dance classes and styles to individuals of all ages. Benni founded the institution in 2020, which unites her love of dancing with his dedication to teaching, assisted by a talented group of teachers. Since each teacher at Ben Dance Academy has a distinct area of expertise, the school is able to provide a variety of dance styles, including ballet, hip hop, salsa, and more. Additionally, they organize neighborhood gatherings and festivities, giving students a stage on which to perform and experience the social side of dance.

The staff of dance instructors at Ben Dance Academy is composed of seasoned, enthusiastic experts who each specialize in different dance styles. These teachers guarantee that students obtain a varied and thorough dance education by combining traditional and modern training.

PAVAN PATEL


Hi everyone, I'm Pavan Patel, I teach Break Dance and Rock & Roll

When I was 7 years old, I began dancing after being requested to participate in my younger sister's dance show and do some gymnastics, which was my first pastime when I was three. I discovered my passion for dancing here, and I enrolled in a dance school to learn ballet and rock & roll. I started going to workshops and Summer Schools on a regular basis. I competed in tournaments, including national championships, most weekends, and I loved performing on stage. I did a lot of shows as well. I made the decision to pursue dance as a career at the age of 16, and I was accepted into full-time schools after a successful audition. In addition to my A Levels, I completed a professional dancing and teaching course at The Mysore School in Mysore. I studied dancing in all its forms.

My first teaching position was in India, where I taught jazz, tap, ballet, and theater choreography. After that, I returned to England and started my own dancing school. My priorities shifted after I became a mother, and I sold the school to spend more time with my kids. I met Benni in 2016, and that's when the journey started! I have a high degree of qualifications and teaching qualifications with the R.A.D & I.S.T.D & I.D.T.A & A.B.D., and I have taken numerous exams in a variety of styles. I enjoy working with kids because I get to see how much fun dance can be and what can be accomplished with commitment and hard work.
